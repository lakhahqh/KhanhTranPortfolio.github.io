Graduation Thesis

Scope of the topic Application of deep learning network to extract drug names from prescription images.

Text Detection and Recognition

Firstly, text areas are detected employing the CRAFT model, then bounding boxes are grouped into potential regions. Then, the bounding boxes that are most likely to contain the drug names are selected as input to the VietOCR model for text recognition.

Medicine Extraction

During the medicine extraction step, a fine-tuned BERT Tokenizer model is used to tokenize the OCR result text into tokens. These tokens are vectorized by utilizing N-grams Bags Of Word, before serving as query vectors in a drug name vector database, which is constructed by HNSW algorithm. Tokens with a cosine measure greater than a certain threshold are returned as potential drug names.
